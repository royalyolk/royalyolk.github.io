About Royal Yolk JS Farms

Royal Yolk JS Farms has been proudly operating in Rawalpindi, Pakistan, for over two years. We specialize in high-quality white layer chicken farming and the production of fresh, nutritious eggs. Our three modern poultry farms are located on Adyala Road, Rawalpindi. We maintain strict hygiene standards, use balanced feeding systems, and follow daily egg collection and health monitoring practices to ensure consistent quality and freshness. At Royal Yolk JS Farms, we are committed to ethical farming, animal welfare, and delivering wholesome white eggs to families and markets across Rawalpindi and beyond.
